Use the New Customer option to enter details of a new customer. You need to do this before you can start saving bill payment data of the customer.

The Chrome extension connected to this software will automatically collect payment details from tneb.org. However, you can use the Bill Payment option to manually enter the payment details of a customer in case of failure or payments made using other browsers or computers.

The Reports section allows you to view quick summaries of payment activity in a time period, as well as detailed information about specific customers
